2. Questions:

Broad Question

What player characteristics and behaviours are most predictive of subscribing to a game-related newsletter, and how do these features differ between various player types?

Specific Question

Can experience, played hours, and age predict if a player subscribes to a game newsletter in players.csv?

Explanation

Subscribe (TRUE/FALSE) is the response, with experience (e.g., "Pro", "Amateur"), played_hours, and Age from players.csv as predictors. I’ll use them to predict subscription and compare how they affect subscribing across experience levels.

4. Methods and Plan

I’ll use K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) to predict if players subscribe (subscribe) using experience, played_hours, and Age from players.csv.

  • Why appropriate?
    • KNN works for yes/no (categorical) classification and can predict subscription by finding similar players based on my predictors.
  • Assumptions?
